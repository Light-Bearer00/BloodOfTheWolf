Blood of the Wolf

In ancient woods, where shadows sleep,

A forgotten pact, the spirits keep.

A whispered legend, ages old,

Of a crimson lineage, fierce and bold.

Through moonlit nights and forest deep,

The echoes of their promise sweep.

Their eyes, like embers, burn so bright,

Guiding their kin through endless night.

A sacred bond, forever sealed,

In every howl, their truth revealed.

With fangs of justice, they still roam,

Guardians of the forest home.
